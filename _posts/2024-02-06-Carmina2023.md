    知其肯綮；泼墨手谈，乐在其中；园艺栽培，略知要领；中医理疗，自幼

I sell my whole soul to you,\\

But with only one flamboyant merit,\\

（2023 Dec.23）\endnote{inspired by the synonymous fragment 2021 Nov.27}
